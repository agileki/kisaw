What is Business? definition and meaning

2020.11.07 12:18


Home Articles Tips Browse by Subject Term of the Day InvestorWords.com - Online Investing Glossary

business

Definition

A commercial activity engaged in as a means of livelihood or profit , or an entity which engages in such activities .
